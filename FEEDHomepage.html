FEED

FEED is a multipurpose cooking app that allows the users to attain numerous amounts of information from things like other's recipes,tutorials, submitting your own recipes, streams by local people, conversion and substitution, and much more. FEED is unique in its one way in that we are the only app that includes the conversion rates with-in the app and have our very own substitution feature that allows for you to substitute ingredients that you do not want or can not have with other ingredients that would not cause issues with the recipe taste and production
